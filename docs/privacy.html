Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026

I Am Worthy is designed to work entirely on your device. This policy explains, in plain language, what that means for your data.

Short version I Am Worthy does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. The app has no servers, no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. It works fully offline and never contacts the internet.

Detailed version

What the app reads from your device

The current date, provided by iOS, used only to select which affirmation to display for the day. This value never leaves your device.

Nothing else. The app requests no system permissions — no access to contacts, photos, location, microphone, camera, health data, notifications, or tracking identifiers.

What the app sends off your device

Nothing. No data of any kind is transmitted by I Am Worthy.

Third-party services

None. I Am Worthy does not include analytics (e.g. Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel), crash reporting (e.g. Crashlytics, Sentry), advertising networks, or any other third-party SDK.

Children's privacy

I Am Worthy collects no data of any kind and is safe to use at any age. The app contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, and no external links to user-generated content. It is not directed at children under 13, but complies with COPPA by virtue of collecting no personal information.

Your rights

Because no personal data is collected, there is nothing to access, export, correct, or delete. This policy is provided to comply with Apple App Store requirements (Guideline 5.1.1) and applicable privacy law — including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), and Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) — even though no personal data is processed.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of I Am Worthy introduces any feature that involves data collection, this policy will be updated before that feature ships, the "Last updated" date above will change, and the change will be summarised in the App Store release notes.

Contact

Questions about this policy or the app can be sent to the address on the support page, or by opening an issue at github.com/trantjustin/i-am-worthy/issues.
