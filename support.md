## FAQ

### The widget doesn't show up on my Lock Screen
Long-press the Lock Screen → Customize → tap the Lock Screen → tap the date
pill above the clock → choose "I Am" from the list. Requires iOS 17 or later.

### The affirmation didn't change today
The app picks one affirmation per calendar day based on your device's local
date. If your time zone changed recently, the message will update at the next
local midnight.

### How do I suggest a new affirmation?
Open an issue on GitHub with the label "affirmation" and your suggestion.

## Privacy
