I Am Worthy

A tiny iPhone app that replaces your Lock Screen date with a daily affirmation.

Instead of Wed 23 Apr, your Lock Screen shows Wed 23 Apr | I am worthy — one short, curated message that changes every day.

How to use it: long-press your Lock Screen → Customize → tap the Lock Screen → tap the date pill above the clock → choose I Am.

Need help?

The support page has setup tips, troubleshooting, and a contact address. The privacy policy explains what data the app uses (spoiler: none).
