Getting started

How do I add the widget to my Lock Screen?

  • Wake your iPhone but don't unlock it.
  • Long-press the Lock Screen, then tap Customize.
  • Tap the Lock Screen option.
  • Tap the date pill above the clock.
  • Choose I Am from the list of widget providers.
  • Tap Done in the top-right to save.

What iPhone and iOS version do I need?

I Am Worthy requires iOS 17.0 or later on iPhone. iPad and Apple Watch are not currently supported.

Troubleshooting

"I Am" doesn't appear in the Lock Screen widget picker

  • Confirm the app is installed and has been launched at least once.
  • Reboot the iPhone — iOS occasionally needs a restart to register a newly installed widget.
  • Make sure you're on iOS 17.0 or later (Settings → General → About).

The affirmation didn't change today

The app picks one affirmation per calendar day based on your device's local date. The message flips at local midnight. If your time zone changed recently, the new message will appear after the next local midnight.

The message is cut off or truncated

The Lock Screen inline slot is narrow, especially on smaller iPhones. If you see a truncated affirmation on your device, please open an issue with your iPhone model so the affirmation list can be tuned.

Can I pick my own affirmations?

Not yet — the current version ships with a curated list. Custom affirmations are being considered for a future release.

Suggesting an affirmation

New affirmation ideas are welcome. Please open a GitHub issue with the label affirmation and your suggestion. Affirmations are kept deliberately short (under 20 characters) so the full "Wed 23 Apr | <message>" fits the Lock Screen date slot.
